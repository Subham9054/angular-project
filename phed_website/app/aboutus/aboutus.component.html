Key Objectives

HED focuses on building a robust water supply infrastructure, promoting water conservation, and improving sanitation standards. Our objectives include reducing waterborne diseases, enhancing community health.

Water Supply Initiatives

PHED implements various initiatives to ensure continuous and safe water supply in both urban and rural areas. We undertake projects like piped water supply systems, borewells, and rainwater harvesting to meet increasing demand.

Sanitation Programs

Our sanitation programs are designed to improve hygiene standards and reduce health risks. PHED promotes toilet construction, waste management, and sanitation education to ensure a cleaner and healthier living environment for all.

Community Engagement

PHED actively engages with communities to raise awareness about water conservation, hygiene practices, and sanitation. We conduct workshops, training, and campaigns to encourage public participation in maintaining water infrastructure.
